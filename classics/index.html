Classics

Rubin Sez…

Rubin is a twentieth century composer and music theory professor at the University of Minnesota Duluth. His talent with music is matched only by his wonderful sense of humor and cast-iron wit. With Rubin Sez… we have documented some of his most profound outbursts of genius.

Leave it to Peter

Peter was my roommate for a year while I was at UMD. Often referred to as Peter with the Hair, Peter needed all that hair to protect the wild ideas that keep coming out of his brain. Leave it to Peter was a day-to-day account of living with this genius. Fire is involved.
