Justin Rubin is a music theory professor at UMD, not to mention an amazing pianist, organist and all-around great person.

Have you been composing a 20th century atonal fugue? He'll take a look at it. Are you late to class? He likely won't notice. Is the lecture material confusing you? He'll laugh with great mirth and explain it to you. Have you spilled scalding coffee in your lap? He'll laugh with great mirth.

Rubin (or Rubinator, as he is known in some social circles) is chock full of excellent advice on a wide range of subjects. What is to follow is only a tiny sampling of this man's awesome wisdom.
